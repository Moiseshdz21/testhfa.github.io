Our Services

What We Do?

Having a variety of options to do in our facilities for everyone to enjoy soccer. The arena was established to enjoy the freedom and show off your talent in a ventilated setting with the option to view your highlights if requested.

Training Ground

The fields are big enough for training sessions for the official H.Town FC indoor soccer team or for the public if rented.

Official H.Town FC Team

We have an official H.Town FC indoor soccer team that competes in the area of all ages. Contact us for more information.

Personal Training

Rent the field and have a personal training session in our facility. You will have to provide the personal trainer.

King of The Hill

Form a 5 side team with 5 minutes each game or score 2 goals. Winner stays on and ties get both teams off the field.

Friendlies

Rent the field for a limited time and feel free to do whatever you want with friends. Contact us for prices.

Tournaments

We host tournamnets for our registered teams and become the best in the area. Contact us for more information.
